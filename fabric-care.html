Fabric Care Guide

Preserving the Grandeur

Our silk sarees are crafted with profound care using delicate golden zari and the purest silk threads. To ensure they remain luxurious heirlooms for generations, we recommend following these care guidelines meticulously.

Washing Instructions

  • Dry Clean Only: We strongly advise professional dry cleaning for all pure silk, banarasi, and tissue silk sarees.
  • Do not use bleach, harsh detergents, or machine wash your silk fabrics.

Storage Advice

Always store your silk sarees in a cool, dry place. Wrap them in pure cotton cloth or muslin rather than plastic covers to allow the fabric to breathe. It is recommended to unfold and change the saree's folding lines every few months to prevent tearing at the creases.
