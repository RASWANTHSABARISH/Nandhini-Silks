Shipping Policy

Domestic Shipping

We pride ourselves on safely delivering our premium silk sarees to customers all across India. Standard domestic orders are processed and dispatched within 2-3 business days.

Delivery Timelines

  • Metropolitan Cities: Beautifully packaged and delivered within 3-5 business days.
  • Rest of India: Delivered within 5-7 business days.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched, a tracking number will be shared with you via email and SMS so you can follow your package's luxurious journey to your doorstep.

International Shipping

We currently ship internationally to select countries. International shipping fees and timelines vary depending on the destination. Customs and import duties, if applicable, are to be borne by the recipient.
